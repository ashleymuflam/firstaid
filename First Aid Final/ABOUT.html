THE TEAM & THE APP

Several countries do not have enough ambulences or emergency numbers people can use if they are ever in an emergenc. In countries where first responders are abundant, people are reluctant to use them because of the high bill and slow response time due to traffic. First Aid hopes to combat this by using the community to help out neighbors in their time of need. Volunteers offer to be "first responders" who are people with cars that are willing to drive people to the hospital in times of emergency. While they are waiting for the first responder and while tey are on route to the hospital, First Aid will provied them with medical procedures they can do while the patient is one their way. The descriptions they give of the injuries will also be used to notify the hospital so that they can prepare in advence. We hope no one if ever in a stituation where they need to use First Aid, but if they do, we hope it is helpful.
